About Me

Hi! I’m Yonni Tompkins — a multi-passionate creative who helps brands grow through authentic content, thoughtful strategy, and polished design.

Over the years, I’ve built a unique blend of skills — from education to UGC content creation, and social media strategy. I love helping businesses bring their ideas to life in ways that feel real, intentional, and uniquely them.

Whether I’m designing a website, creating short-form videos, or managing brand partnerships, my focus is always on connection and growth. I believe your brand should feel like you — and I’m here to help make that happen.

When I’m not behind my laptop, you’ll probably find me planning new projects, collaborating with inspiring people, or figuring out the next step that moves everything forward.
